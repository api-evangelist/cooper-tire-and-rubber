# Cooper Tire & Rubber (cooper-tire-and-rubber)

Cooper Tire & Rubber Company is an American manufacturer of replacement tires for passenger cars, light trucks, SUVs, and commercial vehicles, founded in 1914 and headquartered in Findlay, Ohio. Cooper was acquired by The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company in June 2021 and now operates as a subsidiary, retaining the Cooper brand alongside Goodyear. The Cooper brand portfolio covers all-season, winter, all-terrain, and sport performance tires distributed through retailers and dealers worldwide. Cooper Tire does not publish a public developer program or REST APIs. Its digital presence is consumer-facing tire selection, retailer locators, and post-purchase tire registration.
